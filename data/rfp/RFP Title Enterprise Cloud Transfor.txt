**RFP Title:** Enterprise Cloud Transformation with AI Analytics
**Agency:** Department of Digital Innovation

## Overview

The agency is seeking a vendor to modernize legacy systems and implement AI-driven analytics in a secure cloud environment.

* AI/ML model development
* Enterprise application modernization
* DevSecOps pipeline implementation
* Cybersecurity compliance (NIST, FedRAMP)

* Experience in cloud architecture and system integration
* Strong AI/ML capabilities
* Proven federal project experience
* Ability to deliver scalable enterprise solutions
