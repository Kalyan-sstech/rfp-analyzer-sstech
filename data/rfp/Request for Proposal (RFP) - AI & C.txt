Request for Proposal (RFP) - AI & Cloud Solutions
1. Introduction
The organization is seeking proposals for implementing AI-driven cloud solutions to enhance
operational efficiency and automate business workflows.
2. Scope of Work
The selected vendor will design, develop, and deploy AI models, integrate with cloud infrastructure,
and ensure compliance with security standards.
3. Requirements
Experience in cloud platforms (AWS/GCP/Azure), AI/ML expertise, data processing, and
cybersecurity compliance.
4. Deliverables
AI solution design, deployment architecture, documentation, and ongoing support.
5. Evaluation Criteria
Technical expertise, past experience, cost, and compliance with government standards.
6. Compliance
The solution must comply with FedRAMP, data security policies, and regulatory requirements.
7. Timeline
Project duration is expected to be 6 months from the date of award.
