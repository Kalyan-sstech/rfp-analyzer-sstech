**Request for Proposal (RFP)**
**Project Title:** AI-Enabled Cloud Modernization and Cybersecurity Enhancement
**Agency:** Federal Civilian Agency (FCA)
**Release Date:** June 2026

---

## 1. Overview

The Federal Civilian Agency (FCA) is seeking qualified vendors to provide services for cloud modernization, AI-driven analytics, and cybersecurity enhancement. The goal is to improve operational efficiency, security posture, and digital transformation capabilities.

---

## 2. Scope of Work

The selected contractor will be responsible for:

* Migrating legacy applications to a secure cloud environment (AWS/Azure)
* Designing scalable system architecture for enterprise applications
* Implementing AI/ML models for data analytics and automation
* Providing cybersecurity services aligned with NIST and FedRAMP standards
* Supporting ongoing IT operations, helpdesk, and system monitoring
* Delivering DevSecOps pipelines and CI/CD automation

---

## 3. Technical Requirements

Vendors must demonstrate:

* Experience in **cloud architecture and system integration**
* Expertise in **AI/ML, automation, and application development**
* Knowledge of **cybersecurity frameworks (NIST, Zero Trust, FedRAMP)**
* Capability to provide **24/7 IT support services**
* Strong experience in **federal government projects**

---

## 4. Business Requirements

* Must be eligible for **Small Business / 8(a) partnership**
* Proven experience in federal contracting
* Ability to collaborate with subcontractors

---

## 5. NAICS Codes

Relevant NAICS codes include:

* 541511 – Custom Computer Programming Services
* 541512 – Computer Systems Design Services
* 541519 – Other Computer Related Services
* 541513 – Computer Facilities Management Services

---

## 6. Evaluation Criteria

Proposals will be evaluated based on:

* Technical capability (40%)
* Past performance (25%)
* Price (20%)
* Innovation and AI capability (15%)

---

## 7. Expected Outcome

The agency expects:

* Improved system performance and scalability
* Enhanced cybersecurity posture
* AI-driven decision-making capabilities
* Efficient IT operations

---

## 8. Submission Deadline

All proposals must be submitted by **July 15, 2026**
